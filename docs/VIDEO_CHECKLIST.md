# Short video checklist

Aim for a concise 5-8 minute recording and keep passwords off screen.

1. Show the repository structure, Dockerfile, tests, pipeline, and registry tags.
2. Run the Podman stack; show healthy containers, your name/college, a conversion, database history, and non-root database identity.
3. Show the resource-measurement results and your container-versus-VM conclusion.
4. Show Swarm nodes, 1/1 database, 2/2 app tasks on different nodes, port 80, and scaling to three.
5. Show Kubernetes nodes, pods on different nodes, services, browser access on port 80, and scaling to three.
6. Close with the Swarm-versus-Kubernetes reflection and one real troubleshooting example.
